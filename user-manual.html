Getting Started

Welcome to the University of Swabi GPA/CGPA Calculator! This tool helps you calculate your Grade Point Average (GPA) and Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) quickly and accurately.

What You'll Need

  • Your department information
  • Semester details
  • Subject names and credit hours
  • Your obtained marks for each subject

Time Required

The entire process takes less than 5 minutes once you have your information ready.

GPA Calculation Guide

Follow these steps to calculate your Semester GPA:

1

Select Your Department and Semester

On the home page, choose your department and current semester from the dropdown menus.

Pro Tip: If you don't see your department listed, contact your department office for assistance.

2

Select Your Subjects

After selecting your department and semester, you'll see a list of subjects. Check the boxes next to the subjects you're currently taking.

Pro Tip: You can select all subjects by clicking the "Select All" button if available.

3

Enter Your Marks

On the GPA calculation page, enter your obtained marks for each subject. The system will automatically calculate your GPA as you type.

Pro Tip: Use the tab key to quickly navigate between input fields.

4

View Your Results

After entering all marks, your GPA will be calculated automatically. You'll see a detailed breakdown of your results, including subject-wise grade points.

GPA is calculated using the formula: GPA = Σ(Grade Point × Credit Hours) / Σ(Credit Hours)

CGPA Calculation Guide

Follow these steps to calculate your Cumulative GPA (CGPA):

1

Navigate to CGPA Calculator

From the GPA results page, click the "Calculate CGPA" button, or go directly to the CGPA calculator page.

2

Add Your Previous Semesters

Click the "Add Semester" button for each previous semester you want to include in your CGPA calculation.

Pro Tip: The system automatically includes your current semester GPA if you came from the GPA calculator.

3

Enter GPA and Credit Hours

For each semester, enter your GPA and the total credit hours for that semester.

Pro Tip: You can find your previous semester GPAs on your official transcript or student portal.

4

View Your CGPA Results

Click "Calculate CGPA" to see your cumulative GPA. The results will show a semester-by-semester breakdown and your overall CGPA.

CGPA is calculated using the formula: CGPA = Σ(GPA × Credit Hours) / Σ(Credit Hours)

PDF Export Guide

You can save your GPA/CGPA results as a PDF document for printing or future reference.

Saving Your Results

After calculating your GPA or CGPA, click the "Download PDF Report" button to generate and save a PDF version of your results.

Printing Your Results

You can print the PDF report or save it digitally for your records. The PDF includes all relevant details in a professional format.

Note: The PDF report does not replace official university transcripts. It is for personal reference only.

Tips & Tricks

Efficiency Tips

  • Use the Tab key to navigate quickly between input fields
  • Bookmark the calculator for easy access
  • Have your transcript handy before starting
  • Use the "Select All" option when choosing subjects

Best Practices

  • For best experience use the website on PC
  • Double-check your entered marks for accuracy
  • Save your results as PDF for future reference
  • Compare your calculated GPA with official records
  • Contact your department if you notice discrepancies

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is this calculator official?

A: This calculator is provided by the University of Swabi for student use, but always verify with your official transcript.

Q: Can I use this on my mobile device?

A: Yes, the calculator is fully responsive and works on all devices.But for better experience use it on PC

Q: What if my department isn't listed?

A: Please contact your department office to have your subjects added to the system.
