Introduction

At University of Swabi's GPA/CGPA Calculator, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our calculator tools.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Data Collection

We collect the following types of information:

  • Academic Information: Department, semester, subjects, grades, and credit hours that you input into our calculators
  • Usage Data: Information on how you use our calculators, including time spent, features used, and navigation patterns
  • Technical Information: Browser type, device information, IP address, and operating system for analytical purposes

How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

Provide Services

To calculate and display your GPA/CGPA results accurately

Improve Our Tools

To analyze usage patterns and enhance user experience

Ensure Security

To monitor and prevent fraudulent activities

Communicate

To respond to your inquiries and provide support

Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

Local Storage: Your academic data is stored locally in your browser and is not transmitted to our servers.

  • All data processing occurs client-side in your browser
  • We don't store your personal academic information on our servers
  • We use industry-standard security practices to protect any metadata we collect
