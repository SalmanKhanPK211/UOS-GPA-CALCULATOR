Hello, Salman Khan is the CS Student at UOS

As a computer science student at the University of Swabi, He passionate about creating solutions that make life easier for fellow students. The UOS GPA/CGPA Calculator was born from his own need to quickly calculate my grades without complicated spreadsheets.

His journey in web development started with basic HTML and CSS, and has grown into a passion for creating interactive, user-friendly web applications. He believe in the power of technology to solve real-world problems, especially in the education sector.

Passion for Web Development

My Journey

My interest in web development began during my first year at university when I created a simple HTML page for login/signup. Since then, I've immersed myself in learning various web technologies including JavaScript, React, Node.js, and more.

What excites me most about web development is the ability to create something that people can interact with and benefit from. There's nothing more satisfying than seeing a project come to life and knowing it's helping others.

Why This Project?

I created the UOS GPA/CGPA Calculator because I saw a need for a simple, accurate tool that students could use to calculate their grades. Many existing solutions were either too complicated or not tailored to our university's grading system.

This project combines my passion for coding with my desire to help fellow students succeed academically. It's been a rewarding experience to see how many students have found it useful.
